Our Divisions

Tanzeel Social Development Fund(TSDF)

A dedicated fund managed by Tanzeel Foundation to support community development programs. The fund is sustained by various philanthropic individuals amd organizations dedicated to community welfare.

Tanzeel Social Service

This branch focuses on providing social services,including healthcare,with plans to establish a modern hospital adhering to islamic principles.

Tanzeel Education Center

The core brach of Tanzeel Foundation, aiming to establish educational institutions spanning from daycare and nursery schools to universities, ensuring high-quality education.

Tanzeel Waqfu

A special branch of Tanzeel Foundation dedicated to receiving, managing, and developing endowerments for the benefit for the of the islamic community.

Tanzeel Emergency

This branch focuses on providing and Giving the immediate help and assistance to any kind of emergency break out uch as hunger, disaster, disease break out and various natural calamities to the people affected with it, so as to provide well and bright life to all victims.

100% Zakat Policy – Every donation goes directly to projects, admin costs are covered separately.
