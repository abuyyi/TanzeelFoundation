About Us

Tanzeel Foundation a non-governmental Islamic organization committed to making a positive impacton society. It was established in January 2021, our primary mission is to invest in education, healthcare, and spiritual nurturing to address the multifaceted challenges that our communities face. With a passion for empowerment and community building, we strive to create a brighter future for all, grounded in the principles of compassion, charity, and education. At Tanzeel Foundation, we believe that by working together, we can bring about meaningful change, uplift lives, and build stronger, more resilient communities.

Our goals & objectives

1. Establish an investment trust, in acordance with islamic (sharia'h) and dedicate all of its proceeds to serve and support the objectives.

2. Help muslims practice islam as a belief, a behaviuor, and a way of life, and invite people to the cause of Allah.

3. Provide and facilitates social services including couselling to thise in need.

4. Cherish the bonds of friendship and understanding between muslims and non-muslims within the framework of islam.

5. Establish programs and facilities to preserve and relate the islamic knowledge to the young muslims.

Growing and Providing Together

We work to make immediate and lasting improvements and medical assistance to the lives of people affected by poverty, hunger and disease and who have no one else to turn to.

Our Divisions

Tanzeel Social Development Fund(TSDF)

A dedicated fund managed by Tanzeel Foundation to support community development programs. The fund is sustained by various philanthropic individuals amd organizations dedicated to community welfare.

Tanzeel Social Service

This branch focuses on providing social services,including healthcare,with plans to establish a modern hospital adhering to islamic principles.

Tanzeel Education Center

The core brach of Tanzeel Foundation, aiming to establish educational institutions spanning from daycare and nursery schools to universities, ensuring high-quality education.

Tanzeel Waqfu

A special branch of Tanzeel Foundation dedicated to receiving, managing, and developing endowerments for the benefit for the of the islamic community.

Tanzeel Emergency

This branch focuses on providing and Giving the immediate help and assistance to any kind of emergency break out uch as hunger,disaster, disease break out and various natural calamities to the people affected with it, so as to provide well and bright life to all victims.

Our Impact

Health and medics

Through various projects that was initiated and conducted by Tanzeel Foundation, we were able to provide medical assistance by providing and encourage the community on the impact of donating blood to blood bank, which could be used to save peoples life, in which people can join and volunteer to blood donation.

Education for Children

Through Nisomeshe Charity Event conducted by Tanzeel Foundation, we were able to distribute school materials to orphans and less privileged children who failed to join school due to their deficity. Through the slogan created by us we reflects the vision of organisation towards the program of the respective year. For three years now, we have assisted a total of 345 students, from primary to higher secondary education levels.

Chamazi Magengeni Mosque

In a line with our mission to serve and support islamic values, Tanzeel Foundation proudly undertook the construction of a mosque in Chamazi Magengeni,Dar es salaam. This initiative was driven by our commitment to providing dedicated place for worship and hub for community engagement which was successfully completed in 2023. The Chamazi Magengeni Mosque is a testament to what we can achieve together in strengthening the bounds of our Ummah and fostering spiritual growth within our community.
